Privacy Policy for InSpot

Last updated: February 7, 2026

The InSpot app (the "Service") is designed with your privacy in mind. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

1. Information Collection and Use

We DO NOT collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • No Registration: You do not need to create an account to use the app.

2. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services (such as Firebase, Google Analytics, or advertising networks) that could track your activity or collect your data.

3. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security settings.
